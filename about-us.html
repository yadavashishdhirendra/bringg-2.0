About Us

Enabling businesses to
reliably deliver

Brring 2.0 is the Logistics Execution Startup in 2022, providing End-to-End logistics solutions to perishable new age products. It has its own temperature-controlled facilities at major cities which are interconnected by Reefer vehicles which ply between destinations on scheduled days. Thus, products can be shipped from any city to any city in the country, all under relevant range of temperature.

Modified Services as Per your Business Needs

Every client has a unique set of requirements, which is why we customise our solutions to the individual business' needs. We remove logistical barriers and open your business to global reach.

We have Quality of Excellence

Speed and trust for you. Your reliable logistics partner

Brring 2.0 Logistics is one of India's leading independent providers of transport logistics services. We provide customers with complete solutions to their Supply Chain requirements to include First Mile, Warehousing, Middle Mile, Distribution and / or Last Mile requirements.

Our business is built around our core values of quality, and reliability. We combine industry knowledge and know-how with best practices to drive efficiencies and stay one step ahead of market demands. We are a fully focused logistics group, headquartered in Mumbai, India. We provide seamless, customised supply chain solutions to our customers through our innovative and cost-effective services.We offer an outstanding end-to-end e-commerce fulfilment service for fast-growing online retailers that improves your flexibility and scalability. We take care of the logistics so that you can focus on your business growth.

Pan India Presence

Brring 2.0 is has it's own temperature-controlled facilities at major cities and is expanding fast. All cities are inter-connected with dedicated Reefer vehicles which ply on planned routes on scheduled days of the week. Thus, manufacturers can focus on production and be assured of getting their goods picked up and delivered as per their requirements.Brring2.0 is an Execution Focused company with an opportunity to become the largest E2E Player in the perishable logistics in India.
